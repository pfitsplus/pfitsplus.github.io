Dr. Sergei Dyda is a Postdoctoral Scholar under [Chao-Chin Yang](/team/yang-chao-chin) at The University of Alabama.
He uses numerical methods to study accretion and outflows in astrophysical disks and compact objects.
He has a broad range of research interests and his past work includes young stars (T Tauri), massive stars, X-ray binaries (XRBs), cataclysmic variables (CVs) and active galactic nuclei (AGN).

- __2021—2024__: Postdoctoral Scholar, University of Virginia
